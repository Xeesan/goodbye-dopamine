About
Built by a Student, for Students

Good Bye Dopamine was born out of frustration with generic productivity apps that don't understand student life. We needed something that handles exams, class routines, money tracking, lend/borrow management, GPA calculation, and focus sessions — all in one place, without switching between a dozen apps.

GBD uses a local-first architecture — your data lives on your device and works completely offline. No loading spinners, no lag, just instant access. When you're online, everything syncs to the cloud automatically via Supabase, with full Row-Level Security ensuring your data stays private.

The built-in AI assistant (powered by Google Gemini) lets you manage your entire dashboard just by chatting naturally — in English or Bangla. Add tasks, track money, settle debts, query your exams, all hands-free. It even roasts you when you procrastinate 💀

The gamification system turns every productive action into XP — complete a task (+20 XP), finish a book (+25 XP), do a focus session (+duration XP). Level up every 100 XP with celebratory toasts and unlock titles. It's server-validated so no cheating. Your discipline, your progress, your bragging rights.
